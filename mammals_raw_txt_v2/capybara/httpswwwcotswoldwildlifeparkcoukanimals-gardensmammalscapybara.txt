 p  strong HABITAT &amp; DISTRIBUTION  strong  br 
Capybaras are semiaquatic mammals found wild in much of South America in densely forested areas near lakes, rivers, swamps, ponds and marshes, as well as flooded savannah and along rivers in tropical forest.  p 
 p  strong WHAT THEY EAT  strong  br 
They have huge front incisors like a rabbits to allow them to eat short grasses which they then grind up using their molars.  p
