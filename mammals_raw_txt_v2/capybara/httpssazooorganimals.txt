 p Walk through the entry point and say hello to our three species of bears: speckled bear and black bear.  p 
 p Take some time to see our flock of Caribbean flamingos or watch as our red ruffed and black and white lemurs jump and play. It’s the perfect start to your zoo adventure.  p 
 p Walk through this majestic area as water flows over the rocks. Our Cat Grotto is home to small cats like the caracal, clouded leopard and black-footed cat.  p 
 p Natural limestone spans the center of the zoo where our rhino, storks and hoofstock roam.  p 
 p The Savanna features an exciting exhibit that is home to our three giraffes, Cosmo, Alan and Brayden, as well as birds and zebras. We also offer daily opportunities to feed at the interactive feeding station.  p 
 p Outpost Amazonia is home to more than thirty species of tropical animals including jaguars, an ocelot, monkeys, bats, fish, and a sloth.  p 
 p Specially designed for kids under five, tiny explorers can dig, climb, get wet (bring your swim diapers), and get dirty as they uncover and discover nature’s wonders.  p 
 p Home to a new habitat and our lions, Big Cat Valley allows guests to get face-to-face with these magnificent animals – making for an unforgettable, unique experience while visiting the zoo.  p 
 p A naturalistic enclosure with ropes and vines give the gibbons opportunity for movement and play.  p 
 p Here you will encounter birds from all parts of the world, including the deserts of the American southwest, coastal beaches, the South American rain forest, the jungles of Asia, and the African bush.  p 
 p See underwater views of hippos and Nile crocodiles! Trek throughout Africa Live! to see okapi, African wild dogs, dwarf mongoose, colobus monkeys, a giant aviary, Nile crocodile, variety of fish and termite mound.  p 
 p The Friedrich Aquarium opened in 1948 and maintains a unique charm for many aquarium enthusiasts. The aquarium is home to an array of both fresh water and saltwater animals.  p 
 p Children of all ages have the opportunity to interact with and learn about goats and pigs in our fun petting zoo open daily.  p 
 p Visit Amphibia! a great diversity of frogs, toads, salamanders, and caecilians from Texas and around the world.  p 
 p Reptiles are some of the most interesting animals on the planet. Come check out our wide array of reptiles from all over the world from snakes to turtles to lizards!  p
