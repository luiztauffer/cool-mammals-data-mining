 p Open grassland, tropical rainforest, always near water.  p

 p The capybara lives in family groups of 10 - 40, near to water, where they swim or wallow during the hottest part of the day. In areas where they are not disturbed they may be nocturnal.  p 
 p 2 - 7 youngsters are born after a gestation of 150 days (5 months). Mating takes place in water.  p 
 p Hunting for food.  p 
 p Captive populations of capybara in British and Irish zoos are being managed co-operatively.  p
