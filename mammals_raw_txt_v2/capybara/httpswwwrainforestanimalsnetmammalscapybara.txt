 p The Capybara can be found in various regions of South 
America and in Panama.  p 
 p Capybaras eat grasses, fruits and water plants.  p 
 p Capybaras live near lakes, rivers and streams.  p 
 p Capybaras are approximately 4 ft. long and can weigh up 
to 100 pounds.  p 
 p The pregnancy period of the Capybara lasts about 5 
months and the litter consists of about 2 - 8 offspring.  p 
 p Capybaras have webbed toes which enable it to be a 
strong swimmer. They can also hide in the water by 
holding their noses above water and submerging the rest 
of their bodies below.  p
