 p About 15 minutes by Port Liner from Kobe Sannomiya. Kobe Animal Kingdom is an animal and plant garden with the theme of "The wonderland where flowers, animals and humans interact together." at Kobe Port Island. In the indoor area of ​​the large green house where bright flowers are blooming whole year round, you can feed and contact with amazing cute animals closely such as capybaras, and be surrounded by photogenic scenes. In the open outdoor area, you can experience riding the camel, feeding penguins or kangaroos, and a lot of fun here. The most popular attraction of Kobe Animal Kingdom is the animal performance, especially bird performances show at beautiful water lily pond is the best. In addition, to reproduce the natural habitat of the animals, there is as few fences and cages as possible in the exhibition area that aimed to reproduce the natural habitat of the animals, and you can watch lively animals.
