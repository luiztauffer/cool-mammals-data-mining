 p African lions live in a number of different habitats: grassy plains, open woodlands, semi-desert areas, even high mountains. They eat anything they can catch and kill, including antelope, zebra and buffalo. Lions usually hunt with members of their family group, often by herding a prey animal towards other lions hidden in the bush. It is the adult females who do most of the hunting.  p 
 p African lions are not endangered but are disappearing from some areas because of human activity and the decline of their prey species.  p 
 p The lion is the only social cat which lives in family groups, or prides, of up to 40 animals.  p
