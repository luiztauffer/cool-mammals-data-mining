 p style="text-align: justify;"  a href="https:  en.wikipedia.org wiki Africa" target="_blank" rel="noopener" Africa  a  is the second largest continent after Asia and is home to a number of wild animals. African habitat ranges from rainforests to savannas and deserts, and its animals vary according to its landscapes.  p 
 p style="text-align: justify;" Rhinoceros, zebras, lions, giraffes, elephants, and tigers can be found in the wilderness of Africa. However, if you want to see these amazing animals, there’s no need to travel to Africa. Simply visit Bali Safari Park in Gianyar, Bali.  p 
 p style="text-align: justify;"  a href="https:  www.balisafarimarinepark.com interesting-facts-about-the-rhinoceros " target="_blank" rel="noopener" Rhinoceros  a  are one of the largest herbivores that weigh up to 1,000kg. There are 5 different  a href="https:  www.balisafarimarinepark.com interesting-facts-about-the-rhinoceros " target="_blank" rel="noopener" species of rhinoceros  a  – the Sumatran rhino, the Javan rhino, the black rhino, the greater one-horned rhino, and the white rhino – and they can all be found in Africa, India, and Indonesia. Unfortunately, only the Javan rhino is left in Indonesia, while their Vietnam species are extinct.  p

 p style="text-align: justify;" Recognized for its pretty black and white stripes, this African wild horse, called a  a href="https:  www.balisafarimarinepark.com what-makes-a-zebra-special " target="_blank" rel="noopener" zebra  a , has many more  a href="https:  www.balisafarimarinepark.com what-makes-a-zebra-special " target="_blank" rel="noopener" interesting features  a . For example, underneath its black and white coat, a zebra actually has black skin covering its body.  p

With its long legs,  a href="https:  www.balisafarimarinepark.com facts-about-the-giraffe " target="_blank" rel="noopener" giraffes  a  can run up to 35 miles per hour!  p style="text-align: justify;" Although one of the  a href="https:  www.balisafarimarinepark.com interesting-facts-about-elephants " target="_blank" rel="noopener" largest land mammals  a , elephants are harmless.  They are known to be one of the  a href="https:  www.balisafarimarinepark.com interesting-facts-about-elephants " target="_blank" rel="noopener" gentlest mammals  a , making strong bonds with humans.  This is probably supported by their fascinating long-term memory. Elephants are able to recognize humans they have interacted with in the past, such as caretakers, zoologists, and scientists.  p

They only meet up during mating season, normally from November to April.  p
