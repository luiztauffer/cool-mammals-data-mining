 p style="text-align: center;" align="center"  em  b  span lang="EN" style="font-size: 14.0pt; mso-ansi-language: EN;" Facebook Fans Choose Ruckus For Baby Zebra Name  span   b   em   p 
 p  span lang="EN" style="mso-ansi-language: EN;" Como Zoo turned over the naming rights of its baby zebra to Facebook fans and the fans have responded; nearly 3,000 times. With the contest ending at noon (CDT) today, and with 42% of the votes, the name “Ruckus” is now bestowed upon the little male zebra. The other names offered in the online contest were ZigZag with 33% of the votes and Mayhem with a respectable 25% of the votes.  span   p 
 p  span lang="EN" style="mso-ansi-language: EN;" Como’s current herd now consists of Ulysses, Minnie, Thelma, Melee, and Ruckus.   span   p 
 p  span lang="EN" style="mso-ansi-language: EN;"  Ruckus was born to Thelma on September 7 and Melee was born to Minnie on July 27. Ulysses sired both foal.   span   p 
 p  span lang="EN" style="mso-ansi-language: EN;" The gestation period for zebras ranges from 10-12 months. A newborn zebra’s stripes will turn from brown to black sometime between the first 9-18 months of life. Como is home to Grant’s Zebras, which are commonly found in the grassy plains of eastern Africa including the countries of Kenya and Ethiopia.  span   p 
 p  span lang="EN" style="mso-ansi-language: EN;" The baby zebra is available for sponsorship. Sponsoring an animal is a unique way to help Como Zoo with its wildlife conservation efforts. Donations support enhanced habitats and efforts to maintain the best care possible for the animals at Como Zoo.   span   p
