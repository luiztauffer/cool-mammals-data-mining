 p Zebra are  part of the equidae family along with horse and donkeys.  p 
 p Every zebra  has a unique pattern of black and white stripes.  p 
 p There are a  number of different theories which attempt to explain zebra’s unique stripes  with most relating to camouflage.  p 
 p Wild zebras  live in Africa.  p 
 p Common plain  zebras have tails around half a metre in length (18 inches).  p 
 p Zebra  crossings (pedestrian crossings) are named after the black and white stripes of  zebras.  p 
 p Zebras run  from side to side to being chased by a predator.  p 
 p Zebras have  excellent eyesight and hearing.  p 
 p Zebras stand  up while sleeping.  p 
 p Zebras eat  mostly grass.  p 
 p The ears of  a zebra show its mood.  p 
 p A zebra named Marty starred in the 2005 animated  film Madagascar.                    p
