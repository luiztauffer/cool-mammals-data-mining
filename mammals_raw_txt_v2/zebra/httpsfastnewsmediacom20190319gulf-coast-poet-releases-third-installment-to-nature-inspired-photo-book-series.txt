 p “Hanging Out with Wild Animals III” unveils the charm of the natural world through the power of wildlife photography and Batavia’s verses. The poems are inspired by her real-life experiences from her home state of Florida’s waters, beaches, woodlands, and parks.  p 
 p The author weaves trivia, humor, information on animal behavior, habitat, and more within her poems. Readers will discover environmental themes throughout this book. Batavia encourages readers to go out to see wild animals in action, and the book includes guidelines for observing animals safely.  p
