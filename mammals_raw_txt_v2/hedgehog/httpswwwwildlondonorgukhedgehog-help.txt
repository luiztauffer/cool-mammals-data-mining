	In 2017 London Wildlife Trust launched a project called  a href="http:  www.wildlondon.org.uk hedgehog" target="_blank" Urban Urchins  a  which aims to map London's hedgehog population and promote awareness of their plight among Londoners. For more information  a href="http:  www.wildlondon.org.uk hedgehog" target="_blank" click here  a .  p

	On this page we highlight some the problems – and solutions – to the decline of hedgehogs in London.  p

	   p

	We know that creating a city with healthy, connected wildlife habitats is the essential first step in helping our local hedgehog population recover. But we can't do it alone - everyone who lives in London has a part to play.  p
