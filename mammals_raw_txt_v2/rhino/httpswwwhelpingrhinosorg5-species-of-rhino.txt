 p There are five species of rhino: white rhino, black rhino, Sumatran rhino, greater one horned rhino (or Indian rhino) and Javan rhino. Due to the demand for rhino horn from Asian countries including Vietnam and China driving an unprecedented level of rhino poaching and loss of rhino habitat, all rhinoceros are facing the very real possibility of extinction, with the black, Sumatran and Javan rhinos listed as critically endangered by the IUCN.  p
