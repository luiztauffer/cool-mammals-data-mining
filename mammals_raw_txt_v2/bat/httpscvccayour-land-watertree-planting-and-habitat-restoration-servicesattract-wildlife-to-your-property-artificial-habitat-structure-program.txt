 p  strong What are artificial habitat structures and why are they important?  strong   p 
 p Artificial habitat structures are constructed features such as bird , bee, and bat boxes that are placed in the natural environment to provide breeding or overwintering space for wildlife. These structures:  p 
 p  b Let CVC help you  b   p 
 p CVC is pleased to offer a wide selection of boxes including those for solitary native bees, bats, songbirds, raptors, owls, and waterfowl. We can also design and construct larger habitat projects such as raptor poles, barn swallow nesting structures, and snake hibernacula.  p
