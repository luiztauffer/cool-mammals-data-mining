 p Dry savannah and open forest.  p 
 p Leaves and shoots of trees and shrubs, climbers, vines, some herbs, flowers, seed pods and fruit.  p 
 p The giraffe walks by moving both legs on the same side of the body together. Its maximum galloping speed is 50 - 60kph (31 - 37mph). Giraffes spend 16 to 20 hours a day feeding.  p 
 p A single calf is born after a gestation of approximately 450 - 480 days (15 - 16 months).  p 
 p Low population numbers due to hunting.  p 
 p Zoo populations are managed to ensure a healthy breeding stock in case a conservation breeding programme becomes necessary.  p
