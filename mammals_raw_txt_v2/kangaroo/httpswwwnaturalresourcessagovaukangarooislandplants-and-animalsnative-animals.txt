 p Kangaroo Island is a unique region and is home to a diverse range of  a href=" files sharedassets kangaroo_island plants_and_animals native_animals wildlife-watch-2015-web.pdf" target="_blank" native animal species  a . Many of these animals are now threatened, so it is important that we conserve their habitats and manage the threats that impact on them.  p 
 p Natural Resources Kangaroo Island have implemented a number of projects and work together with  a href="https:  www.naturalresources.sa.gov.au kangarooisland get-involved volunteering volunteering-opportunities" title="volunteer" volunteer  a  groups to maintain, restore and recover the native animal populations on the island.  p
