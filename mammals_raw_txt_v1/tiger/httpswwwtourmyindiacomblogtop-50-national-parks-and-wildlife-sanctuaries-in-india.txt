Adjoining Kabini Lake it forms a vital animal passage that runs through neighbouring Bandipur National Park. Despite sharing the same wildlife, it has fewer visitors than Bandipur, making it all the more appealing and of course less congested. Nagarhole features a good blend of dense jungle and open meadows along the river bank, which makes for fantastic wildlife-watching.
