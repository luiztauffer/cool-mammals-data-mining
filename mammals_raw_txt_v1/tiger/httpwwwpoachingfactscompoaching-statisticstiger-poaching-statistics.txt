 p style="text-align: justify;"  span style="font-family: 'times new roman', times, serif; font-size: 14pt;" India and many Asian nations have a relationship with the tiger going back hundreds, perhaps thousands, of years. Many cultures in Asia have a long history of believing the tiger has supernatural or restorative powers, making the animals valued for their essentially all of their parts.  span   p
