 p  span id="docs-internal-guid-f63b5bed-67cd-80e1-8573-89ea996342c7"  a href="http:  mulupark.com " Gunung Mulu National Park  a   span  !----  in Borneo features several caves, including Sarawak Chamber (the largest cave chamber in the world). Across the park, the caves are inhabited by more than 12 species of bats, though the  a href="https:  www.weather.com travel news spectacular-caves-world-photos-20130626" most famous spot to see bats is Deer Cave  a  !---- , home to over 3 million bats that roost on the roof of the cave. The cave can be visited by three kilometer hike; it is  a href="http:  www.mulunationalpark.com show-caves-deer-langs.php" also located close to the smaller Lang’s Cave, also home to bats  a  !---- .   p

 p  span id="docs-internal-guid-f63b5bed-6bc4-e15a-af0b-45257f040ebb" Every night in the summer, the largest urban bat colony emerges from under Austin’s Congress Avenue Bridge. The   span  a href="http:  www.batcon.org index.php our-work regions usa-canada protect-mega-populations cab-intro" colony is made up of approximately 1.5 million bats  a  !---- , who inhabit the crevices under the bridge that make ideal bat roosts. Each night, the  a href="http:  www.smithsonianmag.com travel best-places-around-world-see-bats-180953185 " critters consume approximately 10,000 to 20,000 pounds of insects  a  !---- , according to The Smithsonian Magazine, providing free pest control for the city. Today, the  a href="http:  www.batcon.org index.php our-work regions usa-canada protect-mega-populations cab-intro" nightly emergence of the bats is an incredibly popular attraction for the city  a  !---- , and every year, over 100,000 people come to witness the spectacle.   p

 p dir="ltr"  span id="docs-internal-guid-f63b5bed-6bf8-cb10-8ea5-2c2131236c38" Located near the tropical rainforests of northern Australia, Cairns is home to huge population of some of the largest species of bats in the world, called   span  a href="http:  www.smithsonianmag.com travel best-places-around-world-see-bats-180953185 " spectacled flying foxes  a  !---- . The most famous of the bats found here is the colony of Brazilian free-tailed bats, which live in the part from April through October. These bats  a href="http:  www.nps.gov cave learn nature bft_bat_outflight.htm" can be seen by visitors during their spectacular outflights from the caverns  a  !---- .
