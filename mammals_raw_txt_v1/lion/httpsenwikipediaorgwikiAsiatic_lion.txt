 p The Asiatic lion is genetically closer to North and West African lions than to the group comprising East and Southern African lions.  p The Asiatic lion's  a href=" wiki Fur" title="Fur" fur  a  ranges in colour from ruddy-tawny, heavily speckled with black, to sandy or buffish grey, sometimes with a silvery sheen in certain lights. Males have only moderate mane growth at the top of the head, so that their ears are always visible. The mane is scanty on the cheeks and throat where it is only 10 cm (3.9 in) long. About half of Asiatic lions'  a href=" wiki Skull" title="Skull" skulls  a  from the Gir forest have divided  a href=" wiki Infraorbital_foramen" title="Infraorbital foramen" infraorbital foramina  a , whereas African lions have only one foramen on either side. The  a href=" wiki Sagittal_crest" title="Sagittal crest" sagittal crest  a  is more strongly developed, and the  a href=" wiki Frontal_bone" title="Frontal bone" post-orbital area  a  is shorter than in African lion. Skull length in adult males ranges from 330 to 340 mm (13 to 13 in), and in females from 292 to 302 mm (11.5 to 11.9 in). It differs from the African lion by a larger  a href=" wiki Tail" title="Tail" tail  a  tuft and less inflated  a href=" wiki Tympanic_part_of_the_temporal_bone" title="Tympanic part of the temporal bone" auditory bullae  a . sup id="cite_ref-Pocock1939_5-2" class="reference"  a href="#cite_note-Pocock1939-5" [5]  a   sup

 p In the Gir Forest, an area of 1,412.1 km sup 2  sup  (545.2 sq mi) was declared as a sanctuary for Asiatic lion conservation in 1965.  p Lions inhabit remnant forest habitats in the two hill systems of Gir and  a href=" wiki Girnar" title="Girnar" Girnar  a  that comprise Gujarat's largest tracts of  a href=" wiki Tropical_and_subtropical_dry_broadleaf_forests" title="Tropical and subtropical dry broadleaf forests" dry deciduous forest  a ,  a href=" wiki Deserts_and_xeric_shrublands" title="Deserts and xeric shrublands" thorny forest  a  and savanna, and provide valuable habitat for a diverse flora and fauna.  p Male Asiatic lions are solitary or associate with up to three males forming a loose  a href=" wiki Pride" title="Pride" pride  a .
